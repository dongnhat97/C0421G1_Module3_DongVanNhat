Doraemon, which I have watched since I was a child, is one of my favorite cartoon. The movie tells about a smart robot cat with a lots of smart gadgets to help a clumsy and lazy student, Nobita. The cat helps him to solve problems arising from school and his friends including fierce friend Takeshi Gouda, the sneaky friend Suneo Honekawa and beautiful Shizuka Minamoto. All the characters together create cool, engaging, and humorous anime episodes. Doreamon is a movie that I’ve watched quiet a lot but not bored. Thanks to the movie, I has learned a meaningful lesson about friendship. All the main characters have flawed personalities, but the group of friends have learned to accept each other regardless.
